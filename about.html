About GridTech

Independent consultancy for utilities and energy organisations modernising operational technology, analytics, and control centres across the electricity value chain.

Who we are

GridTech Innovations UK Limited is a specialist consultancy focused on the electricity sector. We work with generation, transmission, distribution, and market-facing teams — as advisors, not as asset owners or vendors of platforms.

Our engagements span targeted assessments through multi-year transformation programmes, always grounded in practitioner experience from live migration work and control room operations.

We help clients plan and deliver SCADA/EMS modernisation, interoperability across vendor estates, operational analytics, and resilient control centre roadmaps aligned with national infrastructure priorities.

Expertise

Where we add value

Core advisory themes that map to the programmes our clients run today.

  • SCADA & EMS strategy Migration planning, vendor evaluation, cutover readiness, and governance for client-led programmes.
  • Interoperability & standards IEC-oriented integration, unified data models, and breaking down silos across the OT estate.
  • Analytics & decision support Turning real-time and historical data into operational insight — strategy, tooling, and adoption.
  • Control centre roadmaps Future-state architecture, technology roadmaps, and operating models for the grid ahead.
